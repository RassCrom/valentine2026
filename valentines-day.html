Mi Amor,
"If you fall in love with the flower, but not the root, you will be disappointed in autumn." Maybe that's why, at least from my side, it took some time to start the mess. I was falling in love with the roots, though a lot of things are ahead to explore and study.
I am not a great writer, although I need to improve my skills for narrative storytelling for the thesis. I also struggle to express my feelings clearly. So to have a reference of what I want to say in this letter I will use your question: "Why do you love me?"
I think I can start off with more obvious things. You are beautiful, you know I enjoy looking at your face and eyes, and the genuine smile appears on my face to the response of your beauty. Since you landed in my mind, now there are 3 things that I can watch endlessly: Your face, The way you start blinking your eyes, and Your Coneja.
I am an overthinker and very often the flow of the thoughts is like a storm in my head but it all dies down once I can feel you, feel your support, feel your love, feel you physically. When you cuddle me, you take over the chaos in my head. You destroy intrusive thoughts, you help me to learn how to live with the fear of losing you, though this fear is humongous for me it means a lot: it means that your an important person in my life and I want to prove it with the time.
Well, for the closing thoughts, I am pretty sure I can write more. When I was about to start writing this love letter, I could think of anything important and didn't know what to say. But once I got into the game, my head was flooded with the things I want to say, some maybe to early, some I want to keep for the next time. So, at the end, answering your question, there could be many reasons, but Love needs no reason, or We love what we love for no reason at all...
